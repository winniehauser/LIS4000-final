There are potential cuts to children's library funding, related to the cuts in the IMLS funding. You can read more about that here.

We are facing a children's literacy crisis in America. Many young readers are behind their peers in reading levels, especially children of color. It is up to children's librarians and teachers to improve these numbers and foster better reading skills amongst children today. You can read more about that here.

The ALA has updated its standards for school libraries (not the same as children's libraries specifically, but important to know anyway). Learn about those updates here.

A librarian in Georgia was fired for sharing in a community display, a book that contained transgender information and imagery. Not even a banned book in the state of Georgia, but a book with "offensive" content. This story is ongoing; you can read about it here.
