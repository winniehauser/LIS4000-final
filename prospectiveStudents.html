Children's Librarianship

Prospective Students

Being a children's librarian can be very rewarding. If you love working with children and encouraging childhood literacy, this could be the role for you. If the idea of needing experience in the field is daunting to you, don't fret. As part of your MLIS program at the University of Denver, you can become a volunteer, an intern children's librarian, or work in a school library to get that necessary experience and really see if this is the best fit for you.

Additionally, there are plenty of student organizations you can join at the University of Denver when in your MLIS program. These will be helpful for networking, practicing instruction with your peers, and staying in touch with those in your program after your graduate. Your MLIS program, peers, and professors will serve as a great foundation on which to build your MLIS and library career!
